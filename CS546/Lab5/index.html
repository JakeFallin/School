Biography

It feels weird to try and summarize my life in a short biography for someone I don't even know, but let's see how this goes

I was born in Summit, NJ. Pretty normal life nothing interesting really happened. I have a brother whose 4.5 years younger than me. I moved to Japan when I was 5 and then England after that for 5 years. We moved back to Ridgewood after a while because my Dad died of a Glioblastoma. Moved around a lot after that to Maryland, Connecticut, but never for long. Ended up back in New Jersey and decided to go to College close by. Now I'm here. In college, I have found a love of programming, working for a startup and then a large investment banking firm. Not sure where life is going to take me after this.
